Pasta Carbonara - The Ultimate

Pasta Carbonara is a classic Italian dish made with pasta, eggs, cheese, cured pork, and black pepper. It originates from Rome and is loved for its creamy yet rich flavor without using actual cream. Traditionally, guanciale (pork cheek) is used, but pancetta or bacon can be substitutes. The sauce is made by tossing hot pasta with beaten eggs and cheese so the heat cooks it into a silky coating. It’s a comforting, quick-to-make meal that balances salty, savory, and cheesy goodness.
